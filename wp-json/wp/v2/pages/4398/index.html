1. We talk<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Before we start working together. we\u2019ll book in some time to have a chat about your project.\u00a0<\/p>

We\u2019ll take a look at your existing site (if you have one) and we\u2019ll go through your ideas for the new site. We can use this time to talk about your goals and objectives so that we have a better understanding of how we can help you.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

2. Questionnaire<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Following on from our initial call, we will send you a website questionnaire to fill out. This will allow us to gather additional information so we know as much as possible about your business before we start designing and building it. The more information we have, the better the finished product.<\/p>

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Once we have all your requirements, we will send you a full proposal for our services, including all the website features you requested. We may also include additional elements that we think would work well for your website. More often than not, the fee for our services is similar to the price we discussed on the call.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

4. Research<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Using the information you provided on the initial call and the questionnaire, we will begin our own research. We will do a deep dive into your world, learning as much as possible about your industry and your competitors. We\u2019ll perform some keyword research to find out what your customers search for online. During this time we\u2019ll look at dozens of different websites, leaving no stone unturned before moving forward with any designs.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

5. Initial concepts<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

We\u2019ll start by sketching out some layouts on a whiteboard. We use a lot of post-it notes and marker pens to come up with a basic wireframe of your pages. This begins the initial framework of your new site.<\/p>

We\u2019ll also discuss the main colours we\u2019ll be using and taking into account what you already have and what we would recommend. We\u2019ll decide on the best use of fonts and where to place your headlines and content for the most impact. We\u2019ll also decide where your calls to action will go and how to ensure they are clearly displayed for your users.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

6. Mockup<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

The next stage of the process is to take all the rough sketches and post-it notes and turn them into a work of art. Your website. We use Adobe Photoshop to pull together all of the pieces from our planning to create a mockup. We\u2019ll position each element one by one and add some content to bring it to life.<\/p>

Chances are the design will change numerous times throughout the mockup stage until we are happy. This is one of the most import steps, and we\u2019ll keep working on it until we have something that looks spectacular, is user-friendly and ensures maximum results.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

7. You see the design<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

This is the fun part for you. This is the first time you get to see the design since we\u2019ve started working together.<\/p>

Once we are happy with the mockup, we\u2019ll send you the homepage design via a video link. In the video, we\u2019ll walk you through the design and talk you through each element one by one. By using a video, you get to see the full design as if it were a real website. We\u2019ll explain why we\u2019ve designed it the way we have and you\u2019ll get to experience it before we move forward with the rest of the project.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t\t

Once you\u2019ve watched the video and had chance to reflect on what you\u2019ve seen you can tell us what you think. You can also ask us any questions, and we can discuss what you want to change, if anything. This is your opportunity to let us know if we\u2019ve missed out any key information so we can add it before moving forward with the build.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

9. Continue with designs<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Once we have the homepage nailed down, we then design the layouts for your other pages. You will generally have 3 unique layouts to choose from for your website. A homepage, an inner page and a blog page. We\u2019ll design the additional pages using Photoshop like we did before. The colours and style will match the homepage, but the structure will be different.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

10. Domain name & Hosting<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

When we are happy with the designs, we then move onto the technical aspects of the process. We will register your domain if you haven\u2019t already done so. If you have already done this, then we will use a temporary domain, which we\u2019ll work on while we\u2019re building your site.<\/p>

Along with your domain name, we\u2019ll also purchase hosting. This will allow us to install a coming soon page and set up your email accounts so you can use them while we\u2019re building your website.<\/p>
